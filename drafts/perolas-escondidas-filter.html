AR(1) recursive

$x_t$ e $y_t$ ... $y_0 = 0$

$$ y_t = x_t + \phi y_{t-1} $$

$$ y_1 = x_1 + \phi y_0 \ y_2 = x_2 + \phi y_1 \ y_3 = x_3 + \phi y_2 \ $$

filter(x_t, filter=phi, method='recursive', init=0)

AR(2)

AR

$$ y_t = x_t + \phi_1 y_{t-1} + \phi_2 y_{t-2} $$

$$ y_1 = x_1 + \phi_1 y_0 + \phi_2 y_{-1}\ y_2 = x_2 + \phi_1 y_1 + \phi_2 y_0\ y_3 = x_3 + \phi_1 y_2 + \phi_2 y_1\ $$

filter(x_t, filter=c(phi_1, phi_2), method='recursive', init=0)

convolution 1 sides=1

$$ y_t = \phi_1 x_t + \phi_2 x_{t-1} $$
